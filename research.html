Research

My research focuses on developing hybrid modeling frameworks that integrate process-based models with machine learning to bridge spatial and temporal scales in land surface processes. I currently focus on combining mechanistic insights from biogeochemistry with data-driven methods to link local observations to regional patterns.
I am also interested in the intersection of complex systems, graph neural networks, and time series analysis.
